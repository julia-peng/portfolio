Minotaur's Maze

The Minotaur's Maze was a simple game that I made with my partner Maya Peski for the culminating project of the ICS3U course.
It was made to follow the classic Greek legend of the Minotaur, a half-man-half-bull who had been cursed to live in Daedalus's maze for all of eternity.
The project used HSA's Console class as the driver for the graphics, with threading and simple input being used for the user's control of the main character, Theseus.
In the project, my role was mainly to do the graphics and high score system. My partner Maya developed more of the project's systems.
Check out the project at its GitHub page.
