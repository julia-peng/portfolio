Travel Log

The Rockies


I went to Banff in the summer of 2022, with my brother, mother and 3 longtime family friends. It was a wonderful, absolutely stunning experience. As we drove towards the national park from Calgary, the outline of the mountains slowly became more defined and, after some times, we were in front of these monumental structures. The sheer scale of the mountains brought a quite calm about the whole place - we were but specks of dust in this world's expanse.

England


England is a country I hold affection for, as I was born two hours outside of Manchester. The last time I visited it was in the summer of 2022, with my brother and father. There, I got to see family that I hadn't seen in over ten years, revisit places from my childhood and reflect on a life that I could very well have lived.

Switzerland


Switzerland, though initially meant as an in-between on our drive from France to Italy, may have been my favourite destination of the whole Europe trip. The sheer drama depicted in the Swiss scenery was just breathtaking. At every turn, mountains could be found in sight - little huts and mountain goats abound in the scenery. Switzerland's natural beauty was just stunning.

The Maritimes


I visited the Maritimes provinces in the summer of 2021 with my mother, brother and two cats in haul. Though the sights may not have been as exciting or historically significant as some of my other destinations, this trip remains one of my favourite. The quiet calm of just seeing isolation in these provinces brought a peace about the whole trip - it was blissfully normal.

France


In addition to my trip to England, my brother, father and I went on a tour of Western Europe. Among these countries was France. Wow - the streets of Paris were simply stunning - France's deep cultural ties to aristocracy and art were shown off in every single street corner. In addition to that, the countryside was nothing to scoff at either - the rolling hills and lush greenery seemed straight out of a period painting.

Italy


Italy was the final destination of our Europe trip. We were determined to make the long drive down to Rome and, once we were there, I was taken aback by the proud display of heritage found in Rome. Even just roaming the streets, the Roman heritage is apparent, with historic monuments scattered generously throughout the city.
