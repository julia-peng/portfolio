Bead Loom

The bead loom was a drawing project with a special twist - the 400x400 loom was to be drawn in with 20x20 squares, in the fewest lines of code possible.
This posed a special challenge, as the design we - my partner, Maya Peski, and I - settled on was quite complicated and was therefore difficult to automate. For example, the process of drawing a pixellated circle using for loops is much more difficult that that of drawing a square.
In addition to the unique challenge posed by the circle, the diagonal elements were also difficult to program. Since they did not follow standard movement in terms of perpendicular length and width, many of the diagonal elements had to be broken down into two parts that, when put together, would form the target shape.
Though this was a challenging project, it was very rewarding and, in my opinion, yielded very good results.
